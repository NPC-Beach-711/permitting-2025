Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2025

The National Petroleum Council (“NPC”, “we”, or “us”) collects limited information from visitors to this site. This Notice explains what we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: such as your name, email address, and organization when you choose to contact us.
  • Automatically collected information: IP-derived geolocation, browser type, device type, pages viewed, referring URLs, and timestamps.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to measure:

  • pages visited and time spent
  • files downloaded
  • browser, device type, and general region

This information is processed in aggregate and is not used by us to identify individual visitors.

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve the website’s usability and performance
  • Respond to inquiries you submit to us
  • Prepare non-identifying usage statistics

4. Sharing and Retention

We do not sell personal information. Aggregated analytics and usage statistics may be shared with government partners, contractors, or working groups who assist us in operating this site or evaluating its reach.

Web logs and analytics data are retained only as long as necessary for security, operational, and reporting purposes and are then deleted or anonymized where feasible.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information collected through this website. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. External Links

This site may contain links to third-party websites, including government agencies, research organizations, and other external resources. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy notices separately.

7. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice or how information is handled in connection with this website, you may contact:
