Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2025

The National Petroleum Council (“NPC”, “we”, or “us”) collects limited information from visitors to this site. This Notice explains what we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: such as your name, email address, and organization when you choose to contact us.
  • Automatically collected information: IP-derived region, browser type, device type, pages viewed, referring URLs, and timestamps.

2. Use of Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to measure:

  • pages visited and time spent
  • files downloaded
  • browser, device type, and general region

This information is aggregated and not used to identify individual visitors.

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve the website’s usability and performance
  • Respond to inquiries you submit to us
  • Prepare non-identifying usage statistics

4. Sharing and Retention

We do not sell personal information. Aggregated analytics and usage statistics may be shared with partners who assist us in evaluating the reach and performance of this site.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect collected information. However, no internet-based system can be completely secure.

6. External Links

External websites linked from this site are not governed by this Notice.

7. Changes to This Notice

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
